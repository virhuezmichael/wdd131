About Me

My name is Michael Virhuez. I was born in San Juan, Argentina, where I currently live with my family, which consists of me, my wife, and my two sons, ages 9 and 6. Although I haven't always lived in San Juan, I have also lived in Ushuaia, Tierra del Fuego, where my wife is from, for 10 years. It's a beautiful place.

I currently work as a Technical Operations Manager for a software company and as a Monitoring Analyst for a banking corporation.

In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer with friends, spending time outdoors with my family, and exploring nature. I also enjoy growing carnivorous plants and garden plants.

Argentina is a vast, natural country located at the southern tip of South America. Its geography encompasses a remarkable diversity of landscapes, from the glaciers and mountains of Patagonia to the subtropical forests of the north and the fertile plains of the Pampas region. This variety is reflected not only in its natural environment but also in the identity of its regions, which preserve their own cultural traditions, forming a unique mosaic within a single country.

Argentine culture is renowned for its vitality and depth. The country has given rise to world-renowned artistic expressions such as the tango and has been the birthplace of prominent figures in literature, music, film, and science. The local cuisine, influenced by European and Creole roots, offers iconic dishes such as asado, empanadas, milanesa, and the traditional dulce de leche. Likewise, mate, a symbol of unity and friendship, occupies a central place in the daily lives of Argentines.
